# Mainstream Starter Packs

These packs are for office workers and students, not agent engineers.

## 1. Meeting-to-Action Pack

### Use when
- meetings generate too many vague notes
- action items are missed
- follow-up emails take too long

### Pain reduced
- unclear next steps
- bloated meeting summaries
- missed owners and deadlines

### Start with
- `specs/validations/val-summary-faithfulness.example.md`
- `specs/validations/val-action-item-completeness.example.md`
- `assets/meeting-summary.template.md`
- `assets/action-review-checklist.template.md`

## 2. Inbox-to-Draft Pack

### Use when
- users spend too much time drafting replies
- AI drafts sound polished but off-tone or wrong

### Pain reduced
- email overwhelm
- low-trust drafts
- overlong responses

### Start with
- `specs/validations/val-tone-intent-fit.example.md`
- `assets/email-draft.template.md`
- `assets/email-review-checklist.template.md`

## 3. Lecture-to-Recall Pack

### Use when
- students overuse summaries and underlearn
- notes feel familiar but do not stick

### Pain reduced
- passive studying
- false confidence
- weak retention

### Start with
- `specs/validations/val-study-grounding.example.md`
- `assets/study-recap.template.md`
- `assets/active-recall-checklist.template.md`

## 4. Reading-to-Notes Pack

### Use when
- users read articles, PDFs, or textbook chapters
- AI summaries save time but blur evidence and interpretation

### Pain reduced
- shallow summaries
- lost citations or source grounding
- hard-to-review notes

### Start with
- `specs/validations/val-summary-faithfulness.example.md`
- `specs/validations/val-study-grounding.example.md`
- `assets/reading-notes.template.md`

## Mainstream Adoption Rule

A mainstream starter pack should help within one workday or one study session.
